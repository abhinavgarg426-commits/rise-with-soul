My Background

Why I Do This Work

I spent 6 years in product management at a large consumer tech company. I was good at it — got promoted to Senior PM by 28, managed a team of 8. On paper, things were working. Except I was sleeping 5 hours a night, hadn't taken a real vacation in 3 years, and my relationship with my family was deteriorating because I'd become someone who only knew how to talk about work metrics.

The breaking point came when I couldn't remember the last time I'd done something that wasn't in service of a deliverable. Not a single afternoon that just felt like mine. That sounds small when I write it — but it was the first time I realized that if I didn't change something, I'd end up exactly where I said I didn't want to be: successful, miserable, and unable to figure out why.

"I didn't leave because I hated the corporate world. I left because I realized I had been making decisions based on what would look good, not what would feel right. That's the exact thing I help people fix now."

I did a coach training program in 2015, not because I wanted to become a coach, but because I needed to figure out my own thinking. Three months in, I realized this was the work I wanted to do permanently. I've been doing it ever since — first part-time, full-time since 2017.

How It Works

Simple Process, Real Structure

No ambiguity about what you're signing up for

Free Discovery Call

20 minutes. We figure out if we're a good fit. I ask you hard questions. You decide if this feels right. No sales pitch.

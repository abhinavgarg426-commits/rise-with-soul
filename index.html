About Me

What Happened Before
I Became a Coach

I've lived the story I help people with. I was a product manager at a fintech company in Gurugram for 7 years. I was good at it. Promoted twice, team of 9, decent package. I was also having panic attacks before every 9 AM standup. Not because I was bad at the job — because I didn't know why I was doing it. I'd gotten so far away from any internal compass that I couldn't tell the difference between a good opportunity and just another thing that looked like it would make me successful in other people's eyes.

I left in January 2017. Spent six months doing very little — reading, walking, talking to people who were doing work they actually cared about. Did a certified coaching program through the International Coaching Federation in 2018. Started taking clients that same year. I've now coached 340+ people across careers, relationships, purpose, and major life transitions.

"I don't believe everyone needs to quit their job and find their passion. I believe most people need to make a decision, any decision, and commit to it fully instead of half-living seventeen options at once."

Certifications: ICF Professional Certified Coach (PCC), psychometric testing (粉尘 Hogan Assessment), 200-hour yoga teacher training (which informs how I think about body awareness and decision-making)

About Neha

I Left a ₹40 LPA
Corporate Job to
Find What Matters

In 2018 I was a Senior Director at a Fortune 500 company in Gurugram. Great salary, corner office, impressive title. I was also deeply unhappy, sleeping 4 hours a night, and treating my family terribly because I was always "too busy."

I did what many people only think about — I quit. Spent 6 months figuring out who I was outside the corporate identity. Read, traveled, went to therapy, did a full ICF-certified coaching program. Started coaching in 2019, and now I help other professionals avoid the same trap I fell into.

ICF certified, background in organizational psychology, trained under Marshall Goldsmith (the world's #1 executive coach). Real world experience, not just theory.
